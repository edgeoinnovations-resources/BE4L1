Activity 4.1: "Our Big Era" — Modern Parallel Essay

The people of Big Era IV lived in a world defined by iron, empires, and new ideas. Centuries from now, what will historians say defined OUR era?

💡 Thought-Starters
  • Iron changed everything then → What technology is changing everything NOW?
  • The Silk Roads connected the ancient world → What connects our world today?
  • Empires organized millions → What structures organize our world?
  • Writing and religions united people → What ideas unite people today?
  • Deforestation was an unintended consequence → What are OUR unintended consequences?
